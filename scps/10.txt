Item #: SCP-10

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures: Armed guards are to patrol SCP-10. Individuals inside SCP-10 should not enter the room without the presence of at least a Level 2 Level 4 researcher with at least 1 Level 3 Level 4 researcher. Any individuals found to be outside of SCP-10 should be forcibly removed into Restricted SCP Storage Storage Storage.

Description: SCP-10 is a collection of almost a thousand internal documents of various secrecy. SCP-10 contains a number of documents that no reasonably reliable researcher could access. The documents cover various areas from the early 19th century, including:

•Legal code issued by the sovereign of primitive Russia
•Bibliographies of the Holy See and Vatican State
•The Foundation's official travel guide to the Americas
•The Foundation's official document on the origin of hominids
•The Foundation's official school curriculum
•The Foundation's official translation of the Standard Russian Necronomma
•The Great Seal of the State of Wesel
•The Foundation's official spelling of the word "iron"
•The Foundation's edition of "The World Book", and the Foundation's official translation of "The World for Sake of the World"

The documents are embedded within the electronic components of the Foundation's network, all of which seem to have been copied from somewhere else in the world, and therefore likely linked to an unknown or a indirectly unknown source. Processes within SCP-10 locate the documents and verify the authenticity of each claim.
