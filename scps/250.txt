Item #: SCP-250

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-250 is contained within a safe at Site-██. Testing with SCP-250 is to be approved only by Level 3 personnel. In the event that any equipment within SCP-250
is damaged in a scenario where SCP-250 suddenly becomes capable of manifesting anomalous objects, SCP-250 is to be returned to Site-██ for further testing. In the event that SCP-250 is unable to immediately yield its anomalous properties, SCP-250 may be returned to its home base for at least a week for removal.

Description: SCP-250 is the collective designation for a number of devices that appear to be based on personal computers and have links to various sites and entities. Each device is unique and unique with a number starting with '0' and varied by settings beyond the typical 1000 to 3000. A list of entities referred to may be found in document 250-IP
