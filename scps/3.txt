Item #: SCP-3

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures: In order to prevent public awareness of SCP-3, the recording of the final words of the Prophet Mohammed (on the night of October 7th) will be played on the local radio station for local populations in the Islamic Republic of Iran. This will be an air-to-air attack by an array of RIAS units, all of which have been created to deal with events such as the one detailed above. Further details will follow subsequent containment efforts.
