Item #: SCP-378

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-378 is kept in a safe environment on the first floor of Site-60, with access to him only through a single doorway. Given that Foundation personnel prefer to remain under the cover of a large sewer, SCP-378 is to be kept on the second floor hall of the site. SCP-378 may be placed in a room at least 4m x 4m with at least two (2) personnel, but only after testing with a live animals.
